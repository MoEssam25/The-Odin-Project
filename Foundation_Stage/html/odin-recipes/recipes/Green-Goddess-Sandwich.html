Odin Recipes

Dressing

  1. 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  2. 2 tablespoons chopped chives
  3. 1/2 teaspoon dried tarragon
  4. 1 anchovy filet
  5. 2 tablespoons toum (Lebanese garlic spread)
  6. 2 tablespoons whole milk plain Greek yogurt
  7. 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  8. salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Sandwich

  1. 2 slices bread
  2. 1 leaf Romaine lettuce, torn in half
  3. 1 handful baby spinach leaves
  4. 2 slices tomato
  5. 4 slices cucumber, or as needed
  6. 2 slices Monterey Jack cheese, or as needed
  7. 1/2 avocado, sliced
