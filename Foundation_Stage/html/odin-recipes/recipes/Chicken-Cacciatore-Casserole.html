Chicken-Cacciatore-Casserole

Description

This chicken cacciatore casserole transforms the classic Italian favorite into a crowd-pleasing one pot baked pasta dish. Chicken, spaghetti, and veggies simmer together in a rich tomato sauce and are finished with melted cheese.


  1. 12 ounces dried spaghetti, broken in half
  2. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  3. 3 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning
  5. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  7. 3 cups chopped cooked chicken
  8. 3 cups chicken broth
  9. 2 cups marinara sauce
  10. 1 (14.5 ounce) can petite diced tomatoes, undrained
  11. 1 cup chopped green bell pepper
  12. 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  13. 1 1/2 cups shredded Italian-blend cheese
  14. 2 tablespoons chopped fresh Italian parsley (optional)
